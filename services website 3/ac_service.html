AC Services Offered

1. AC Servicing (Jet & Foam Cleaning)

Our deep cleaning service uses a high-pressure jet and a specialized foam solution to thoroughly clean the indoor and outdoor units. This removes accumulated dust, dirt, and mold, leading to improved cooling efficiency, better airflow, and healthier air quality. The service includes a detailed inspection of the filters, coils, and drain line to ensure everything is functioning optimally. We also provide a final check of the unit's performance to ensure maximum comfort.

Price: ₹499 (per unit)

2. Gas Top-Up

A gas top-up is essential when your AC's cooling performance decreases due to a refrigerant leak. Our technicians will first perform a comprehensive leak check using advanced tools to identify and fix any issues before refilling the refrigerant gas (R22/R32/R410A) for both split and window ACs. Post-service, we test the cooling performance to ensure your AC is running efficiently and your home is cool again.

Price: ₹1499 – ₹2199 (based on AC type)

3. AC Installation

Get a professional and hassle-free installation for your new split or window AC unit. Our service includes secure mounting of the indoor and outdoor units, proper copper pipe setup, and all necessary electrical connections. We ensure a clean and efficient setup with up to 3 meters of free piping to get your new AC running perfectly from day one, maximizing its lifespan and performance.

Price: ₹1199 onwards

4. AC Uninstallation

Safely remove your AC unit with our trained professionals. This service includes the careful dismantling of both indoor and outdoor units, securing the refrigerant gas for future use, and ensuring the area is left clean. Our technicians follow strict safety protocols to prevent any damage to your property or the AC unit, making the process smooth and worry-free.

Price: ₹599

5. AC Wet Servicing

Recommended for AC units that have not been serviced in a long time or are extremely dirty. Our wet servicing provides a full, deep cleaning with water to remove stubborn dust, mold, and clogs that can significantly impact airflow and cooling. This service is especially effective for heavily contaminated units and is recommended every 4–6 months to maintain peak performance and air quality.

Price: ₹699

6. AC PCB Repair

The PCB (Printed Circuit Board) is the brain of your AC. If your AC is not turning on, the compressor isn't running, or the display is showing errors, a faulty PCB may be the culprit. Our experts will diagnose the issue and either repair the existing board or replace it with a new one. All repairs come with a warranty for your peace of mind.

Price: Starts at ₹1499

7. AC Noise or Vibration Fix

Unusual noise or vibrations from your AC can indicate a mechanical issue. Our technicians will troubleshoot the source of the noise, which could be a loose fan, misaligned parts, or faulty bearings. We will perform the necessary alignments and part replacements to ensure your AC runs smoothly and quietly, restoring your home's tranquility.

Price: ₹299 – ₹799 depending on issue

8. Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)

Secure your comfort year-round with our AMC package. It includes three comprehensive services (a mix of wet and dry cleaning), priority support during peak season, exclusive discounts on any required repairs, and regular health check-ups to prevent future breakdowns. This is the perfect solution for hassle-free AC maintenance throughout the year.

Price: ₹1499/year
