Mechanic Services Offered

1. General Vehicle Servicing

Comprehensive check-up and maintenance for your two-wheeler or four-wheeler. Includes oil change, air filter cleaning, brake inspection, coolant top-up, and battery check. Ensures smooth performance and longevity of your vehicle.

Price: ₹799 (Bike), ₹1299 (Car)

2. Engine Diagnosis & Repair

Our expert mechanics use advanced diagnostic tools to identify issues in your vehicle's engine. Services include timing adjustment, noise troubleshooting, spark plug replacement, and minor engine repairs to restore optimum performance.

Price: ₹999 onwards

3. Brake & Clutch Service

Get your brake pads, clutch plates, and cables inspected and replaced if needed. We ensure complete safety and smooth functioning with quality parts and expert fitting. Suitable for both manual and automatic vehicles.

4. Battery Check & Replacement

Facing frequent vehicle start issues? We provide battery voltage check, terminal cleaning, and replacement with branded batteries. Includes free installation and old battery disposal. 1-year warranty available on new batteries.

Price: ₹699 (check), ₹2399+ (replacement)

5. Vehicle Pickup & Drop

Too busy to visit the garage? Book our contactless pickup & drop facility for service or repairs. Your vehicle is handled with utmost care by trained professionals with complete transparency throughout the process.

Price: ₹199 (one-way), ₹349 (round trip)

6. AC Repair & Gas Refill

Restore your vehicle’s cooling with our AC service. Includes compressor check, refrigerant refill, and blower cleaning. Ensures fresh air, improved cooling efficiency, and better mileage. Applicable to both cars and large two-wheelers with AC.

Price: ₹1499 onwards

7. Emergency Breakdown Assistance

Stranded on the road? Our rapid-response team is available to assist with on-spot flat tire repair, jumpstart, fuel delivery, and towing service. Quick resolution to get you back on the road without delay.

Price: ₹299 – ₹999 depending on issue

8. Annual Vehicle Care Plan (AMC)

Year-round peace of mind with 3 scheduled services, priority support, discounts on spare parts, and doorstep assistance. Designed for proactive vehicle owners who want hassle-free performance and extended vehicle life.

Price: ₹1799/year (Bike), ₹2799/year (Car)
